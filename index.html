MakeMyTrip UX Case Study

Simplifying Travel Booking Experience

Problem Approach Solution Impact

Problem Statement

Users on MakeMyTrip often face difficulty in making quick and confident booking decisions due to overwhelming choices, lack of transparency, and complex checkout flows.

  • Too many options → decision fatigue
  • Hidden pricing reduces trust
  • High drop-offs during checkout
  • Poor personalization

Approach

  • User research to identify pain points
  • Competitor analysis (Booking, Airbnb, Expedia)
  • Breaking journey into stages: Search → Compare → Book
  • Focused on simplicity, transparency, personalization

Solution

  • Smart Recommendations: Personalized suggestions
  • Comparison Tool: Side-by-side view
  • Transparent Pricing: No hidden fees
  • Quick Filters: Budget, Family, Business
  • Streamlined Checkout: Fewer steps
  • Trust Indicators: Reviews, ratings, badges

Impact

  • Faster user decision-making
  • Reduced booking drop-offs
  • Improved trust and satisfaction
  • Higher conversion rates
